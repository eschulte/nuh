> "Willie Baptist teaches us that 'Movements begin with the telling of untld stories.' With this new quarterly newsletter we intend to elevate the plight, fight, and insights of the poor and dispossessed. Everything in our society tells us that poor people can only be one of two things: criminals (objects of fear) or charity cases (objects of pity). In contrast to these lies, we recognize that poor people, when organized together across our many differences, have the power to bring about a fundamental transformation of society."

> "Our goal in this issue and future newsletters is to tell the stories of struggle, to share and develop analysis and understanding of the crises and oppressions we suffer under, and to help break down barries between us to **UNITE THE POOR.**"
